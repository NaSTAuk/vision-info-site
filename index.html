Back for 2017

NaSTA-Vision is your stations opportunity to show your abilities to the rest of NaSTA.
Whether it be through VT Production or Live Broadcasting,
you can showcase talent from your institute through the medium of film.

Go Live!

If you have the ability to do a multicamera live broadcast great! We want you to live stream a band for the show, get your angles planned and your shot list prepared!

Only a camera?

If you have at least a camera then great! You can put together a VT to showcase an act from your institue of study. So get your track recorded and your music video cut!

Awards

There are 2 awards up for grabs, Best Live Performance and Best Edited Performance, The winners of Best Live Performance will also be the next hosts for NaSTA-Vision 2018.
